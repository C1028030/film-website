1. Clair Obscure: Expedition 33



This game is my favourite game of all time. It set a new standard for games that I think will not be matched in a long time, and has truly brought back the love of video games for me.




2. Sonic Adventure 2



This game was my favourite game from childhood, and it holds a special place in my heart. It aged like fine wine. It is the best Sonic game to this day.




3. Bloodborne



It was either Bloodborne or Elden Ring, but I lean more towards Bloodborne for it's very unique setting, and style of play. It is a Soulsborne game that encourages aggressive play, and has some of the best bosses in the series.
